# Enderite

**The ultimate end-game progression mod for Minecraft!**

Enderite adds a powerful new material from The End that surpasses even Netherite, complete with unique armor abilities, a mysterious Void dimension, and challenging progression.

## Features

### Two Tiers of Power

**Enderite Tier**
- Upgrade your Netherite gear using Enderite Ingots found in The End
- Each armor piece grants unique abilities:
  - **Helmet**: Endermen won't aggro when you look at them
  - **Chestplate**: Built-in Elytra flight
  - **Leggings**: 8% chance to dodge projectiles
  - **Boots**: Reduced fall damage
- Complete tool set with improved durability
- All items are fireproof

**Void Infused Tier**
- The ultimate tier with enhanced abilities
- **12% projectile dodge** on leggings (up from 8%)
- **Helmet** grants Luck effect for better loot
- **Boots** grant Weaving effect
- Trade-off: Items have Curse of Vanishing

### The Void Dimension

A dangerous new dimension with:
- Unique structures and challenges
- Special resources needed for Void Infusion
- Custom fog effects and atmosphere

### Enderite Golem

Craft a powerful guardian using Enderite Blocks:
- 100 hearts of health (2x Iron Golem)
- Devastating attack damage
- Custom cracked texture as it takes damage

### Progression System

30+ custom advancements tracking your journey from your first Ender Eye to becoming a Void Master. Discover secret achievements and complete unique challenges across dimensions.

## Requirements

- **Minecraft**: 1.21.4
- **Fabric Loader**: 0.16.14+
- **Fabric API**: Required

## Compatibility

This mod is designed for Fabric and uses mixins for some features. Should be compatible with most other mods, though interactions with other gear/dimension mods may vary.
